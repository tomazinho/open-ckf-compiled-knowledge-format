# Source Provenance

The source books are not redistributed in this repository. Case provenance is recorded at the case level.

- **Avaliação Sem Mistério**: 9 cases
- **Orientações Baseadas no Cérebro para Transformar Ensino em Aprendizagem**: 11 cases

## Adaptation method

Each case paraphrases and condenses a local pedagogical principle into a benchmark item with raw, annotated, and compiled representations. Page ranges are recorded to support author review and future traceability.
