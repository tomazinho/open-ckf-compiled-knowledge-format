# Legal Knowledge Compilation

**Paper:** *Da Compilação Documental à Compilação Jurídica: Comparação entre dois Compilers CKF aplicados à Lei nº 15.211/2025*

This paper compares a general-purpose CKF compiler with a domain-specialized legal CKF compiler using the same legal source document: Brazil's Law 15.211/2025, the Digital Child and Adolescent Statute.

## Why this matters for CKF

Legal and regulatory knowledge depends on scope, jurisdiction, applicability, obligations, prohibitions, exceptions, sanctions, temporal validity, institutional authority, and article-level traceability.

The study shows that legal-grade knowledge compilation is not only about generating more structure. It is about generating structure aligned with normative risk and controlled legal inference.

The legal compiler emphasizes:

- article-level traceability;
- jurisdiction-aware metadata;
- legal-system calibration;
- safer normative interpretation;
- structure aligned with compliance and legal-agent use cases.
