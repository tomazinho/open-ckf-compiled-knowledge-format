# Grand Synthesis

**Paper:** *Do Texto Bruto ao Conhecimento Compilado: Arco Evolutivo do CKF como Tecnologia de Compilação de Conhecimento*

This paper consolidates the CKF research program from the initial motivating failure mode through the compiler, pipeline, robustness, reproducibility, model sensitivity, and application phases.

## Why this matters for CKF

This is the recommended single entry point for readers who want to understand the full research arc behind CKF: the problem, the representation hypothesis, the compiler architecture, the engineering constraints, and the path toward practical applications.

The synthesis frames CKF as a technology of knowledge compilation, not merely as another JSON/YAML document format.
