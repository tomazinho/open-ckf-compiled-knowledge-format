# Research

This directory collects the research papers, benchmark protocols, technical reports, and synthesis documents behind Open CKF.

The materials are organized as a research timeline: from the motivating failure mode, through CKF compiler evolution, robustness studies, model sensitivity analyses, synthesis papers, and domain-specialized applications.
