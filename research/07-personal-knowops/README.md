# Personal KnowOps and Obsidian

**Paper:** *KnowOps no Obsidian: Uma Arquitetura Local para Conhecimento Pessoal Compilado, Operacional e Agentivo*

This paper applies CKF concepts to personal knowledge operations, especially workflows involving Obsidian, structured notes, local vaults, sidecar CKF files, personal knowledge bases, and operational memory.

## Why this matters for CKF

CKF is not limited to enterprise RAG or formal policy documents. It can also support personal knowledge operations by turning notes, procedures, decisions, and source material into structured, traceable knowledge packages.

This paper introduces Obsidian as the human authoring layer, CKF as the compiled representation layer, and KnowOps as the operational runtime for knowledge.
